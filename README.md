

## 📊 Project Title & Headline
Healthcare Insight Dashboard
Transforming Healthcare Data into Actionable Business Intelligence using Power BI

## 📝 Short Description
This project focuses on analyzing healthcare data to uncover meaningful insights related to financial performance, patient trends, and regional efficiency. The dashboard enables stakeholders to make informed, data-driven decisions by providing a clear view of key operational and business metrics in the healthcare sector.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* Power BI – Data visualization & dashboard creation
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Advanced calculations & KPIs
* Power Query – Data cleaning & transformation
* Data Modeling – Relationship building & schema design

## 📂 Data Source
Dataset collected from Kaggle, containing healthcare-related data such as patient records, revenue, costs, and regional performance.

## ✨ Features & Highlights
* Interactive dashboard with dynamic filters and slicers
* KPI tracking for revenue, cost, and profit trends
* Region-wise performance comparison
* Patient and service-level insights
* Monthly and yearly trend analysis
* Clean and intuitive UI for better user experience

## 📌 Business Problem
Healthcare organizations often struggle with fragmented data, making it difficult to track financial performance, monitor patient trends, and identify high-performing regions or services. This lack of visibility can lead to inefficient decision-making and missed growth opportunities.

## 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

* Provide a centralized view of healthcare performance
* Enable quick analysis of revenue and cost trends
* Identify top-performing regions and services
* Support strategic decision-making with real-time insights

## 📊 Walkthrough of Key Visuals
* Financial Overview Panel: Displays total revenue, cost, and profit with trend indicators
* Regional Analysis Map/Chart: Highlights performance differences across locations
* Service-Level Breakdown: Shows which healthcare services generate the most revenue
* Time-Series Analysis: Tracks monthly trends to identify growth patterns and seasonality
* Interactive Filters: Allows users to drill down by region, time period, and service type

## 📈 Business Impact & Insights
* Identified **high-revenue regions**, enabling focused resource allocation
* Highlighted **cost-heavy areas**, supporting cost optimization strategies
* Revealed **top-performing services**, helping improve service offerings
* Improved overall **decision-making efficiency** through real-time data visibility
* Enabled stakeholders to quickly spot trends and take proactive actions
